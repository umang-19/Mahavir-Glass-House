Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common queries about our services.

How long does windshield replacement take?

A standard windshield replacement usually takes about 1.5 to 2 hours. We also advise allowing the vehicle to sit for an additional 1 hour to let the urethane adhesive cure, ensuring safe drive-away conditions.

Do you provide insurance support?

Yes, we work with all major insurance companies and provide cashless claim processing facilities. Our team will assist you with all the necessary paperwork to make the process hassle-free.

Do you use genuine glass?

Absolutely. We only use OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) standard glass that meets all safety and quality regulations. Your safety is our top priority.

Which car brands do you support?

We provide glass solutions for all major car brands including Maruti Suzuki, Hyundai, Tata Motors, Mahindra, Honda, Toyota, Kia, MG, Volkswagen, Skoda, BMW, Mercedes-Benz, Audi, and more.

Do you offer emergency services?

Yes, we understand that a broken windshield can be an emergency. Please call our hotline number and we will arrange for a swift replacement or secure your vehicle as quickly as possible.

What are your charges?

Pricing varies depending on your car model, year, and the specific piece of glass required (windshield, side glass, etc.). We offer highly competitive pricing and free quotes. Contact us with your vehicle details for an exact estimate.
